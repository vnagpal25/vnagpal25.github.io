About Me

I am Vansh Nagpal, and I am 3rd-year undergraduate student at the University of South Carolina Honors College. I am pursuing a dual-degree in Computer Science (BS) and Mathematics (BS) with a minor in Data Science. Currently, I am an undergraduate research assistant at the SyReX Lab, working under Dr. Sanjib Sur to study the applications of 5G networks and mmWave-based infrastructure. Specifically, I am currently working on a project with the goal of being able to accurately sense and classify pedestrians and vehicles utilizing transmitted 5G mmWave signals with a Deep-Learning based approach. I was awarded the Magellan Scholar Award to pursue this research for the 2023-2024 academic year. I am eager to learn and explore the many applications of Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
